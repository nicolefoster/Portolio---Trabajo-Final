Wonderland

A visual system developed for the release of a new album by the band Pompeya. The identity draws inspiration from the bold, contemporary aesthetics of Berlin and Copenhagen — sharp compositions, dynamic layouts, and unapologetically expressive forms. At its core lie primitive 3D shapes, reinforcing the raw, rhythmic quality of the design. The result is a light, free-flowing visual language that mirrors Pompeya's sound: effortless, vibrant, and full of movement.
